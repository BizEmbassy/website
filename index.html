FAQs

Q: How do you guarantee 3 customers?

A: Authority-backed outreach + problem-focused approach + systematic follow-up = predictable results. If you prepare and follow-up thoroughly, but don't get 3 qualified commitments, we will refund you.

Q: What if my English isn't perfect?

A: Perfection is overrated, but you must handle concise, focused meetings in English with ease. We will help you prepare.

Q: How do you select companies?

A: We only choose serious, proven B2B startups or service providers ready to actively participate in international expansion.

Q: Why the Netherlands specifically?

A: English-speaking, tech-forward, and gateway to Europe. Plus, we have established embassy relationships there.

Q: Can companies outside of CEE apply?

A: Yes, if you are EU-based. But you will have to come to Bratislava for the workshop. It's crucial that you meet the other companies in your cohort in person.
